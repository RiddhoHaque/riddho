Riddho R. Haque

Ph.D. Student, College of Information & Computer Sciences, University of Massachusetts Amherst.
Research area: Optimizations, Data Management
Advisors: Peter J. Haas, Alexandra Meliou.

I am a PhD student at the DREAM lab at UMass Amherst. I work on creating scalable in-database algorithms that allow users to make risk, diversity and fairness-aware decisions from very large volumes of data. The tools I am currently working on can potentially be used for a variety of tasks, from making risk-constrained stock market investments and automatically detecting privacy infringement by ML classifiers to selecting an unbiased and diverse set of social media responses. Previously, I was a software engineer at the Pixel Camera System Software team at Google, where I worked on optimizing the speed, memory and power usage of Pixel's on device image processing algorithms.
